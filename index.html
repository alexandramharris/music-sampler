POP MUSIC COVERS

BY ALEXANDRA HARRIS

This page is dedicated to four covers from pop albums. These samples include music from The Weeknd, The 1975, Haim and Lorde.

The 1975 is an English pop rock band led by Matthew "Matty" Healy. The band released a recent album in 2022. The Weeknd, Abel, is a popular Grammy-winning artist. Haim, based in Los Angeles, is comprised of sisters Este, Danielle and Alana. Ella, known as Lorde, has released three albums, including Pure Heroine, Melodrama and Solar Power (which was actually good—a hill I will die on).
